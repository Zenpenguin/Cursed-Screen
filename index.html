Privacy Policy

Cursed Screen — Last updated: March 6, 2026

In short: Cursed Screen does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information stays entirely on your device.

1. Information Collection

Cursed Screen does not collect any personal information. The app does not require account creation, does not ask for your name, email, or any identifying details, and does not use analytics or tracking of any kind.

The only data the app processes is your cumulative daily screen-on time, which is calculated locally on your device and never leaves it.

2. Permissions Used

The app requests the following Android permissions to function:

  • Usage Access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS) — Used to read device usage statistics so the app can determine your daily screen-on time. This data is processed locally and is never transmitted off-device.
  • Display Over Other Apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW) — Used to render the visual overlay on screen edges. The overlay is non-interactive and never blocks touch input.
  • Foreground Service — Used to keep the screen-time tracker running reliably in the background. This service only tracks elapsed screen-on time locally.
  • Receive Boot Completed — Used to restart the tracking service after device reboot so tracking remains continuous without manual intervention.

No permission is used to access contacts, camera, microphone, location, files, or any other sensitive data.

3. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This includes:

  • Daily cumulative screen-on time (resets at midnight)
  • User preferences and settings

No data is stored on external servers, cloud services, or any remote infrastructure. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data.

4. Third-Party Services

Cursed Screen uses Google Play Billing to process a one-time in-app purchase. This transaction is handled entirely by Google Play and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

The app does not integrate any other third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or tracking services.

5. Children's Privacy

Cursed Screen is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children. Since no personal data is collected at all, there is no risk of children's data being gathered or misused.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
