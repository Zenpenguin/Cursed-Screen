Privacy Policy

Cursed Screen — Last updated: March 31, 2026

In short: Cursed Screen does not collect personal data. The only data transmitted from your device is anonymous crash reports (if enabled) to help us fix bugs.

1. Information Collection

Cursed Screen does not collect any personal information. The app does not require account creation, does not ask for your name, email, or any identifying details.

The only data the app processes is your screen-on time, which is calculated locally on your device using its own timer and never leaves it. The app does not read or store your app usage history.

2. Crash Reporting

Cursed Screen uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports when the app encounters an error. This data helps us identify and fix bugs. Crash reports include:

  • Stack traces (the technical details of what went wrong)
  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version
  • A random installation identifier (not linked to your identity)

Crash reports do not include your screen time data, app usage, personal information, or any content from your screen. This data is processed by Google Firebase and is subject to Firebase's Privacy Policy.

3. Permissions Used

The app requests the following Android permissions to function:

  • Usage Access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS) — Used to detect which app is currently in the foreground. This is needed for the App Whitelist feature (hiding the overlay on apps you choose) and for hiding the overlay during phone calls. No usage data is stored — the app checks the foreground app in real-time and immediately discards the result.
  • Display Over Other Apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW) — Used to render the visual overlay on screen edges. The overlay is non-interactive and never blocks touch input.
  • Accessibility Service — Used solely to display the overlay in a way that does not interfere with other apps. The accessibility service does not read your screen content, does not monitor keystrokes or passwords, and does not collect or transmit any data. It requests zero accessibility events and has no access to window content. This permission is optional — the app works without it.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — Used to display a persistent notification while the background service is running. Android requires this notification to keep the service alive. No promotional or marketing notifications are ever sent.
  • Foreground Service — Used to keep the screen-time tracker running reliably in the background. This service only tracks elapsed screen-on time locally.
  • Receive Boot Completed — Used to restart the tracking service after device reboot so tracking remains continuous without manual intervention.
  • Billing — Used to process the one-time in-app purchase through Google Play.

No permission is used to access contacts, camera, microphone, location, files, or any other sensitive data.

4. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This includes:

  • Daily cumulative screen-on time as measured by the app's own timer
  • User preferences and settings (theme, mode, grace period, etc.)
  • App whitelist (list of apps you have chosen to exclude from the overlay)
  • Onboarding and warning acknowledgment flags

No personal data is stored on external servers, cloud services, or any remote infrastructure. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data.

5. Third-Party Services

Cursed Screen uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing — Processes the one-time in-app purchase. Handled entirely by Google Play and subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — Collects anonymous crash reports to help improve app stability. Subject to Firebase's Privacy Policy.

The app does not integrate any advertising networks, user analytics platforms, or tracking services.

6. Children's Privacy

Cursed Screen is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.
